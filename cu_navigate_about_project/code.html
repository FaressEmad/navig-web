Our Mission

To empower every student, faculty member, and visitor at Cairo University with the tools to navigate our sprawling campus effortlessly, ensuring that no one ever misses a lecture or a meeting due to the complexity of our historic environment.

visibility

Our Vision

A campus where physical boundaries are navigated through a digital lens, creating a seamless flow of people and ideas across the entire Cairo University ecosystem.

THE CHALLENGE

Getting Lost shouldn't be part of the Curriculum.

Cairo University spans over hundreds of acres with complex building layouts, overlapping departments, and historic corridors that can confuse even the most seasoned students. We realized that traditional static maps were no longer enough for a 21st-century academic environment.
