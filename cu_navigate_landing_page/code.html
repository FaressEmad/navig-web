Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the location tracking? expand_more
CU Navigate uses a hybrid location system combining GPS, Wi-Fi fingerprinting, and campus-wide beacons to provide accuracy within 2 meters, even inside large faculty buildings.
Do I need an internet connection? expand_more
While navigation works best online, you can download the full campus map for offline use. Search and routing will still function perfectly without data.
Is my privacy protected? expand_more
Absolutely. Your location data is processed locally on your device and is never stored on our servers. We only collect anonymous telemetry to improve campus routing.
